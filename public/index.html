Research Watcher

Stay on top of academic research that matters to you

Research Watcher delivers personalized, daily digests of academic papers based on your interests. Track your favorite authors, venues, and keywords across multiple academic databases.

  • Daily ranked digests tailored to your research interests
  • Track papers by authors, venues, and keywords
  • Aggregate results from OpenAlex, Semantic Scholar, Crossref, and arXiv
  • Smart scoring based on citations, venue prestige, and recency
  • Free to use with Google or email sign-in
Status: Limited Alpha! Phase 2 complete - collector and search are live.
